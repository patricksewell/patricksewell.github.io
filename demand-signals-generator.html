Demand Signal Generator

This tool generates one round of demand indicators for a supply chain experiment. Participants (supply chain managers) observe up to N noisy demand signals and choose an inventory level. Each indicator is drawn from Normal(D, σ²), where D is the true demand. Their plan succeeds if the cumulative average of observed signals falls within a coverage level δ of D.

Generate one sequence draws a single random (or seeded) sequence. Generate until constraints met keeps trying until the sequence satisfies the default difficulty constraints. Top 10 from 1000 candidates evaluates 1 000 sequences and returns the best-scoring ones. Milestone rows at n = 5, 8, 10, 12, 16 are highlighted in the table.
